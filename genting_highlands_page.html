Genting Highlands

Introduction

Cameron Highlands is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Titiwangsa Mountains of Malaysia, renowned for its cool climate, lush tea plantations, and breathtaking landscapes. Also, it is famous for its flora and, as well as tea and strawberries, you will also find a huge number of species of roses here.

What can we do?

Theme Park

Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park

Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park is an exhilarating amusement destination located within the Resorts World Genting complex, offering a multitude of thrilling rides, games, and entertainment options for visitors of all ages.

Genting SkyWorlds Theme Park

Genting SkyWorlds Theme Park is an upcoming and highly anticipated amusement park nestled in the scenic Genting Highlands of Malaysia, promising a unforgettable entertainment experiences for visitors of all ages.

Sky Casino

Considered one of the region's premier casino, the Genting Casino is the first gaming space to open at the resort and remains a hot favourite among visitors.

Accessible attractions

Chin Swee Cave Temple

The Chin Swee Cave Temple is a captivating religious site located in the Genting Highlands. This temple is renowned for its intricate architecture and serene surroundings, offering visitors a tranquil retreat and a glimpse into Malaysian spirituality.

SkyAvenue Complex

The SkyAvenue Complex is a vibrant shopping and entertainment destination nestled within the heart of Genting Highlands in Malaysia, this shopping complex offering a diverse array of retail, dining, and leisure experiences.
