Cameron Highlands

Introduction

Cameron Highlands is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Titiwangsa Mountains of Malaysia, renowned for its cool climate, lush tea plantations, and breathtaking landscapes. Also, it is famous for its flora and, as well as tea and strawberries, you will also find a huge number of species of roses here.

What We Can Do?

Flora Park

Cameron Highland Flora Park is a captivating botanical haven nestled in the heart of the Cameron Highlands, Malaysia.

The Sheep Sanctuary

The sheep sanctuary in Cameron Highlands offers a charming retreat where visitors can enjoy the company of adorable sheep while surrounded by the serene beauty of the highland landscape.

The Sheep Sanctuary tickets are very cheap, only RM8 for adults and RM5 for children, and a pack of sheep feed is included!

Mossy Forest

It's like a surreal forest from a movie, giving a feeling of a dreamlike fairyland scene. Moreover, this is the border between Pahang and Perak states. When you climb to a high point, you can see a panoramic view, with layers of clouds painting a beautiful picture!
